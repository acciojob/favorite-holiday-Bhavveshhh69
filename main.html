Antarctica


Antarctica is a continent at the southernmost part of the world, covered in snow and ice. It experiences six months of darkness and six months of daylight each year — isn’t that amazing?

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Lemaire Channel – a stunning narrow passage surrounded by glaciers.
  2. Deception Island – an active volcanic island with a natural harbor.
  3. Port Lockroy – a historic British research station and museum.
  4. South Shetland Islands – a popular site for penguin and seal spotting.
  5. Colossal Penguin Colonies – home to thousands of emperor and Adélie penguins.

Must-Try Foods

  • Hot Chocolate – perfect for staying warm in freezing weather.
  • Seafood Chowder – a creamy soup made from local Antarctic waters.
  • Frozen Fruit Dessert – nature’s ice cream made from preserved fruits.
